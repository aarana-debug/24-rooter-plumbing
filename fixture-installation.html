About This Service

What We Do

We install faucets, sinks, showers, bathtubs, shower valves, hose bibs, and any other plumbing fixture in kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms, and outdoors. If you have already purchased your fixture and need it installed correctly, we do that. If you need guidance on what to buy and where, we can source quality fixtures at trade pricing and install them for you.

Fixture installation often involves more than just connecting supply lines — we also handle the drain assembly, p-trap, shut-off valves, and any needed pipe modifications. We get it done right the first time so you do not deal with leaks after the fact.

Common Questions

Fixture Installation FAQs

Can I supply my own fixture and have you install it?
Absolutely. Many customers purchase their own fixtures from big-box stores or specialty plumbing suppliers and hire us to install. Just confirm the fixture is compatible with your existing plumbing configuration and we will handle the rest.
How long does a faucet replacement take?
A standard kitchen or bathroom faucet replacement typically takes one to two hours. More involved jobs like shower valve replacement or new sink installation can take two to four hours depending on access and existing pipe condition.
Do you handle all the connections — drain, supply lines, everything?
Yes. We handle the complete installation including supply line connections, drain assembly, p-trap, and any needed shut-off valve replacement. You get a finished, working, leak-free installation.
