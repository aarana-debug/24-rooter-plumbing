Introduction

Welcome to 24 Rooter & Plumbing Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care, transparency, and respect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you interact with us through our website, services, or communications.

By using our website or providing us with your information, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you contact us, request a quote, or use our services:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number(s), including numbers provided for SMS communication
  • Service address or location
  • Any additional information you voluntarily share with us

How We Collect Information

We gather information through the following methods:

  • Online contact and quote request forms on our website
  • SMS and phone communication consent processes
  • Direct phone or in-person conversations
  • Cookies and standard web tracking technologies when you visit our website

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to serve you better. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Respond promptly to your inquiries and service requests
  • Schedule, confirm, and follow up on appointments
  • Send appointment reminders or service-related updates via SMS (if you have opted in)
  • Improve the quality of our services and customer experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Data Security

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. We employ appropriate technical and organizational safeguards — including data encryption, secure servers, and restricted access controls — to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we work diligently to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely without risk. We encourage you to contact us directly if you have any concerns about the security of your information.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

We may share your information only with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services (such as scheduling or communication tools), or with legal authorities when required by law.

Important: Phone numbers collected through our SMS consent process, and any other contact information provided for SMS communication, will never be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that inaccurate or outdated information be updated
  • Deletion — Request that your personal data be removed from our records

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at 24rooterplumbing@gmail.com or call us at (626) 322-1538.

Message frequency varies depending on the nature of your service and communication preferences. We will not send unsolicited or excessive messages.

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. International messages may incur additional charges. Contact your carrier for details.

You can opt in to receive conversational SMS messages in the following ways:
