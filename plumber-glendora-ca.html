We Know Glendora

Proud to Serve the Pride of the Foothills

Glendora is one of the San Gabriel Valley's most beautiful foothill cities — known for its charming downtown village, excellent schools, and well-maintained neighborhoods. It's a community that takes pride in its homes, and we take pride in keeping those homes' plumbing in top shape.

Many Glendora homes, particularly those in the Village area and older established neighborhoods, were built in the 1950s–1970s and feature original plumbing systems that are aging. We have extensive experience upgrading these systems while respecting the character of Glendora's historic properties.

We serve all of Glendora including the foothill neighborhoods, the Glendora Village area, and properties along Glendora Mountain Road and the San Gabriel foothills.

  • Glendora's foothill terrain can cause unique water pressure issues — we diagnose and fix PRV problems
  • Mature trees near older homes frequently cause root intrusion in sewer lines
  • Historic Village-area homes may have original galvanized or cast iron pipes
  • Mountain Road properties with well water or unique supply systems — we handle them
  • Senior citizen and law enforcement discounts always available
  • Free estimates on all Glendora jobs, no obligation

What are common plumbing issues in Glendora homes?

Tree root intrusion in sewer lines is very common due to Glendora's mature landscaping. Aging galvanized pipes in Village-area homes, water pressure issues in foothill properties, and water heater sediment from hard water are also frequent issues we see.
