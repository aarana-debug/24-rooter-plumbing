About This Service

What We Do

A plumbing emergency does not follow a schedule. Burst pipes can release hundreds of gallons per hour. Sewer backups create serious health hazards. Gas line leaks are life-threatening. That is why 24 Rooter & Plumbing Inc. maintains a 24/7 emergency line staffed by real plumbers — not an answering service.

Our trucks are stocked with the parts needed to handle the most common emergencies on the spot. We will assess the situation, give you a clear upfront price, and get to work immediately. We serve Claremont and all surrounding Southern California cities with some of the fastest response times in the area.

Common Questions

Emergency Plumbing FAQs

Do you really charge the same rate at 2am?
Yes — we do not add after-hours, weekend, or holiday surcharges. The price you get at 2am is the same as the price during business hours.
How fast can you arrive in Claremont?
Our base is in Claremont, so we typically arrive within 30 to 60 minutes for emergencies in the 91711 area. Response times to surrounding cities vary but we always aim to be there as fast as possible.
What counts as a plumbing emergency?
Any situation causing active water damage, a health hazard such as sewage backup, or a safety risk such as a gas leak is an emergency. When in doubt, call us — we will help you assess the situation over the phone at no charge.
