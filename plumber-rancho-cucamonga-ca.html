Rancho Cucamonga Plumbing FAQs

How does hard water affect plumbing in Rancho Cucamonga?

Rancho Cucamonga's water has a very high mineral content (hardness). Over time, calcium and magnesium deposits build up inside water heaters, reducing efficiency and eventually causing failure. They also build up in pipes and on fixtures. Annual water heater flushing, quality water softeners, and regular maintenance extend the life of your plumbing significantly.
