Hydro Jetting

Snaking clears a clog. Hydro jetting cleans the entire pipe. Using water pressure up to 4,000 PSI, we blast away years of grease buildup, mineral scale, and root intrusion that a standard snake can't touch.

Hydro jetting is the preferred method for restaurants, commercial kitchens, and any home with recurring clogs. It leaves pipes in like-new condition and dramatically reduces future blockages.

  • Up to 4,000 PSI water pressure
  • Removes grease, scale, sediment, and roots
  • Ideal for restaurants and commercial kitchens
  • Restores full pipe diameter and flow
  • Extends the life of your drain system
  • Camera inspection before and after available

Whole-Home Solution

Repiping & Pipe Replacement

Galvanized steel pipes installed before the 1970s corrode from the inside out — restricting water pressure, discoloring water, and eventually failing altogether. If your home has galvanized pipes, repiping is the most cost-effective long-term fix.

We repipe homes using modern copper or PEX piping, which is durable, flexible, and resistant to corrosion. Most whole-home repipes are completed in one to two days with minimal wall damage.

  • Galvanized pipe removal and replacement
  • Copper and PEX repiping options
  • Whole-home and partial repiping
  • Improved water pressure and quality
  • Minimal wall and floor disruption

Water Quality

Water Filtration & Softeners

Southern California is known for its hard water — high in calcium and magnesium that builds up in pipes, clogs fixtures, shortens water heater life, and leaves residue on dishes and skin. A whole-home water softener or filtration system solves it at the source.

We install and service whole-home water softeners, sediment filters, and reverse osmosis drinking water systems. We'll assess your water quality and recommend the right solution for your home and budget.

  • Whole-home water softener installation
  • Salt-based and salt-free softener systems
  • Sediment and carbon block filtration
  • Under-sink reverse osmosis systems
  • Water quality testing and assessment
  • Filter cartridge replacement and maintenance
