Serving Walnut's Desirable Neighborhoods

Walnut is a highly desirable residential community in the eastern San Gabriel Valley, known for its excellent schools, diverse community, and well-maintained homes. Many residents have lived here for decades and take tremendous pride in their properties.

Walnut's housing stock is primarily from the 1970s–1990s, with many homes featuring copper supply lines and cast iron or ABS drain systems. While generally reliable, these systems are reaching the age where professional inspection and maintenance can prevent costly emergencies.
