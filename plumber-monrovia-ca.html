Local Knowledge

We Know Monrovia

Serving Old Town Monrovia and Beyond

Monrovia is a historic foothill city with a charming downtown and some of the most beautiful older homes in the San Gabriel Valley. Many of these homes — especially those built before the 1970s — feature original plumbing that requires experienced hands.

We've worked extensively throughout Monrovia's neighborhoods including the Old Town area, the North Monrovia foothill estates, and communities near Mayflower Village. We understand the local infrastructure and common plumbing issues specific to Monrovia homes.

Monrovia's proximity to the San Gabriel Mountains means mature trees with roots that regularly invade sewer lines. Our hydro-jetting and sewer camera services help Monrovia homeowners stay ahead of these issues.

  • Historic homes in Old Town often have cast iron drain lines — we inspect and reline them
  • Tree root intrusion from mature landscaping is a top issue — we use camera inspection to diagnose
  • Foothill homes sometimes experience fluctuating water pressure — we diagnose and fix PRV issues
  • Aging galvanized supply lines common in pre-1970 Monrovia homes — safe copper/PEX replacement available
  • Senior citizen discounts honored — just mention it when you call
  • Available nights and weekends with no additional emergency surcharge
Common Questions

Monrovia Plumbing FAQs

How fast can you respond to a plumbing emergency in Monrovia?

We typically arrive within 45–75 minutes for emergency calls in Monrovia. We're available 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

My old Monrovia home has slow drains everywhere — what causes that?

Widespread slow drains in older homes usually indicate a main line issue — often root intrusion or buildup in the main sewer line. We use a camera to inspect the line and hydro-jetting to clear it efficiently.

Do you charge extra for weekend or after-hours calls in Monrovia?

No. We believe in fair, upfront pricing. We don't add surprise surcharges for nights, weekends, or holidays. The quote you get is the price you pay.

Can you replace galvanized pipes in my 1950s Monrovia home?

Yes — this is one of our specialties. We repipe older homes using modern copper or PEX piping, dramatically improving water quality, pressure, and reliability. Free estimate provided before any work begins.
