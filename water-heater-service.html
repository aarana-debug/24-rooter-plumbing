Common Questions

Water Heater FAQs

How long does a water heater replacement take?
Most standard tank replacements take 2 to 3 hours. Tankless installations typically take 4 to 6 hours depending on venting and gas line work required.
Should I repair or replace my water heater?
If your water heater is more than 10 to 12 years old and needs a significant repair, replacement is usually the better investment. We will give you an honest recommendation based on the specific issue and the age of the unit.
Do tankless water heaters require a bigger gas line?
Yes — tankless units draw more gas on demand than tank models. We assess your existing gas line and upgrade it if necessary as part of the installation at a fair upfront price.
