About This Service

What We Do

Most hardware store drain cleaners just punch a hole through a clog and leave the buildup behind. Our professional snaking and hydro jetting equipment clears the entire pipe — removing grease, hair, scale, and root intrusion that drain chemicals cannot touch.

We also use drain cameras to locate the exact source of recurring blockages. In older Claremont and SoCal neighborhoods, tree root intrusion into clay or cast-iron drain lines is extremely common — we specialize in diagnosing and clearing these.

Common Questions

Drain Cleaning FAQs

How do I know if I need drain cleaning vs. hydro jetting?
Snaking handles most standard clogs. Hydro jetting is recommended for recurring blockages, grease buildup, or when snaking is not fully effective. We will recommend the right method after assessing your drain.
Can you clear a main sewer line clog?
Yes — main line clogs are one of our most common jobs. We use heavy-duty sewer snakes and hydro jetting equipment designed specifically for main line clearing.
How often should I have my drains cleaned?
Most homeowners benefit from a professional drain cleaning every 1 to 2 years as preventive maintenance. Homes with older pipes, large trees nearby, or frequent clogs may need it more often.
